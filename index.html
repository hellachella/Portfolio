About Moi

I'm Chelle Lorenzen, a creative person working and living in Ottawa, Canada. I'm a baby ninja with HTML, CSS, JQuery, Javascript, looking to become a full time grown up ninja.

But wait, there's more! I also like to make things move. Screen things. I was recently lucky enough to be chosen for an Applied Arts award for motion graphics, and I was thrilled to work with a crackerjack team of pros at McMillan Agency on poster design for the Ottawa International Animation Festival as an illustrator. Seeing something I made on the back of a bus because it was supposed to be there for a change was kind of a thrill.
